Frequently Asked Questions
How is the Risk Score calculated?
The score is derived from a local Transformer-based model (FLAN-T5) combined with clinical heuristics across all vital trends over the last 24 readings.
Is this data real?
This is a simulation portal using synthetic data for demonstration of AI monitoring capabilities.
How do I export records?
Click the "Export Data" button on the Dashboard to download the complete history as a CSV file.
